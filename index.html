The Product Smart Warranty Tracker is a mobile and web-based one stop solution for keeping track of reminders, events and tasks in one place. One of the primary feature is "ability to add/create reminders, events or tasks using voice-based feature", which entails and adds value to the customers. This App helps reminding products service maintenance or warranties, time to pay bills, remind elderly to take medicines etc.

The app can take from the user the event name, date and time of the event. Store the details to the database and alert the user 10 min before the event. Events are based on alternating calendar dates & frequency (user input). Notification/reminders will be sent via Text Message and email notifications. All the information Cute Reminder stores for you is classified depending on how it relates to a time. This comes with lots of useful features, allowing you to save your time, increase productivity and focus on your job and life.
